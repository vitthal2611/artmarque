Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Interior design costs in Pune typically range from ₹350 to ₹2,500+ per sq. ft. depending on the design style (basic to luxury) and scope of work. At Artmarque, we charge a fixed design fee — not a percentage of the project cost — so your fee stays the same whether you upgrade materials or not. Use our Budget Calculator above for a quick estimate.

A typical full-home interior project takes 8–12 weeks from design finalization to handover. The design phase (concepts, 3D visuals, approvals) takes 2–3 weeks. Execution depends on scope — a modular kitchen can be done in 3–4 weeks, while a full 3 BHK turnkey project usually takes 8–10 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project.

No — and this is one of our biggest differentiators. Most designers charge 10–15% of the project cost as their fee, which means the more you spend on materials, the more they earn. We charge a fixed design fee agreed upfront. This means you're free to choose premium materials without worrying about inflated designer fees.

Absolutely. You're free to bring your own vendors or use ours. We share comparative quotes from multiple vendors transparently so you can choose who to work with and pay them directly. This ensures you get the best value with zero hidden margins on our end.

Yes. Every project includes 3D renders and mood boards so you can visualize the final space before a single nail is hammered. We present 2–3 design concept directions and iterate until you're 100% happy. Execution only begins after your explicit approval.

We serve all of Pune and PCMC — including Wakad, Baner, Aundh, Hinjewadi, Kharadi, Viman Nagar, Kothrud, Karve Nagar, Chinchwad, Pimpri, Ravet, Pimple Saudagar, and Hadapsar. If your location isn't listed, reach out — we likely cover it.

We offer 2 years of post-execution maintenance support. If anything installed by our team needs attention — a cabinet hinge, a paint touch-up, a fixture issue — we handle it. Most designers disappear after handover; we stay available.
